ORANGE LAZARUS

An Orange Lazarus is a dangerous thing. Consumed too quickly, it can shut down a drinker's central nervous system in 1.2 seconds. Oppenheimer created it looking for beauty and perfection, but instead created a "hedonistic nectar of greed and power."" It's "too tempting, too delicious, too cold."" You think you drink it? "No, it drinks you."

The second album by Smiling Strange took advantage of a long weekend in the Boston Suburb of Concord. This is the first attempt to create a cohesive whole as opposed to individual songs. The album features music primarily of Evan and Kyle Rosse, with added help by Sophia Rosse, Katherine Sartiano, and Mikey Corenthal

Mixing done on a home PC while in Dresden Germany
